FAQ

Do you provide alcohol?

Typically the client purchases alcohol, and I provide a detailed shopping list. Ask about add‑ons for specialty ingredients.

How far do you travel?

DMV area (DC, MD, VA). Travel fees may apply depending on distance and timing.

What do you need from me?

Event date, location, guest count, hours of service, and if you want signature cocktails or a simple setup.

Deposit & cancellation?

A non-refundable deposit is required to secure your date. Remaining balance is due by the event day. Cancellations must be made at least 7 days in advance. Deposits may be transferred to a new date if available.
